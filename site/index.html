What is TriForge AI

AI that thinks
before it answers

Most AI tools give you one answer from one model. TriForge AI runs three of the world's most powerful AI models simultaneously — OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, and xAI Grok — then synthesizes their answers into a smarter, more reliable response.

It lives on your desktop, not in a browser tab. Your data stays local. Your API keys stay private. No middleman. No subscription required to get started.

TriForge is built for people who use AI seriously — entrepreneurs, builders, researchers, and anyone who wants more than a chatbot. It's your personal think tank, available the moment you open your laptop.

Beyond conversation, TriForge can plan and execute multi-step tasks autonomously, switching between three operational AI personas via Forge Profiles, accepting voice commands hands-free with offline wake-word detection, and pairing with your phone via QR code — all running locally on your hardware.

Business users unlock the Income Operator — an approval-first autonomous agent that identifies income opportunities, proposes time-boxed experiments, and executes approved actions across eight income lanes (digital products, affiliate content, AI music, and more). Every action requires your sign-off. The ForgeHub Skill Catalog and curated Starter Packs get you live in minutes with pre-built templates, guided installation, and a full audit ledger of every decision made.

How It Works

Up and running
in minutes

No technical setup. No cloud accounts to manage. Just download, add your API keys, and start thinking bigger.

1

Download & Install

Download the DMG for Mac (Apple Silicon). Drag TriForge AI into your Applications folder. Launch it. The splash screen greets you with a voice introduction while the app initializes.

2

Grant Permissions

A one-time setup wizard walks you through enabling the features you want — file access, printer control, voice input. You're always in control of what TriForge can do.

3

Add Your API Keys

Go to Settings and paste in your API keys from OpenAI, Anthropic, and/or Google. Keys are stored locally on your machine using encrypted storage — they never leave your computer.

4

Start Thinking Together

With all three models active, type your question. TriForge runs it through every AI simultaneously and delivers a synthesized, consensus answer. Your personal think tank is live.
